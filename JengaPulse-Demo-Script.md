## 📋 PRODUCTION NOTES

**Screen recording tips:**
- Record at mobile size (375×812 or similar) for a phone feel — or use a phone frame overlay
- Use real (or seeded) data in the app — fake names like "James Kamau", "Wanjiku Fitness", "Benson Mwangi" feel local and authentic
- Don't rush the taps — let each screen breathe for 1–2 seconds before moving on

**Voiceover tone:**
- Confident, not salesy
- Speak like you're talking to a friend who owns a gym, not pitching an investor
- Kenyan accent is a strength here — keeps it local and trustworthy

**Music:**
- Low background beat — something clean and modern, not hype
- Slightly louder in Scene 1, fade under during feature walkthrough

**What to seed in the app before recording:**
- 8–10 leads with different stages in the pipeline
- 15–20 members (a few with expiring memberships, a few with low visit streaks)
- 3–4 class sessions with varying attendance
- 4–5 Google reviews (mix of 5-star and one 3-star)
- 1 active campaign, 1 completed campaign
- A few message threads
